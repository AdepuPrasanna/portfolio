BizEase — Local Biz Digitizer
A mobile-first Micro-SaaS platform that helps kirana stores, cafes, and pharmacies digitize their entire business — billing, inventory, customer credit tracking, and supplier invoice processing — all from a smartphone with no POS hardware. Built on Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Twilio WhatsApp integration and Tesseract.js OCR.

AI-powered bill scanner
Shop owners snap supplier invoices and Tesseract.js OCR automatically extracts product names, quantities, unit prices, and GST values — directly updating inventory. Low-confidence extractions are flagged for a manual correction workflow before saving.
Instant WhatsApp receipts
After each billing transaction, a formatted digital receipt is sent to the customer's WhatsApp via Twilio API — no paper printing, no email setup. Customers get itemized bills with totals, discounts, and business contact instantly on their phone.
Khata credit ledger
A complete credit and payment ledger for each customer — tracks outstanding balances, records partial payments, and maintains a full transaction history. Real-time balance updates on every billing or payment event.
Secure multi-tenant SaaS
Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) ensures each shop can only read and write its own data. JWT authentication via Supabase Auth, a full Admin Console for platform-wide management, and a service_role key for safe server-side database access.
